Disc Golf Party

For my master’s theis I pitched an idea for a mobile application surrounding disc golf. Through 20 weeks of user research, ideation and user testing I created "Disc Golf Party". At the end of the project in June 2022, I launched the app myself on Google play store and it has since gotten 500+ downloads. Check it out here.
